starting* вҖ” non-blocking, no checkbox, no extra tap. `[i]` opens a full privacy sheet. `consentAcceptedAt`

readable by staff who know to look. If any indicator is red, K1 shows a muted banner instead of
`TAP TO START` вҖ” see [System States](./phase-3-wireframes-system-states.md).
